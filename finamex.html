Finamex

Web Development

Finamex, Casa de Bolsa, is one of the first Mexican brokerage houses to enter the international markets. Currently, it has a presence in the United States through Valores Finamex Internacional Inc., a US Broker-Dealer that has been registered with NASD since 1990.

At Finamex, Casa de Bolsa, my main role was to translate the prototypes of the new institutional image into CSS, acting as a UX Engineer, as well as updating and proposing flows with the feedback from QA, iterating to find solutions to improve the interface, seeking to make it more intuitive and playful for our users.
As a UX Engineer, I used the IONIC 6 framework for the app and Bootstrap for the web version.
