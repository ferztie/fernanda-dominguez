Fernanda Domínguez

Lentes Púrpura

Web Development

"Lentes Púrpura" is a feminist association and consulting firm.

It is a comprehensive project where we developed the logo, designed the identity, managed the project development and generated all UX design, including UXR, iteration and testing by the foundation's team and its users. The most interesting aspect has been its gradual growth, which translates into innovation and new tools, such as its online store, its OnDemand workshops management system, its donation manager, etc.
The project continues with the aim of continuing to grow and expanding its functionalities.
