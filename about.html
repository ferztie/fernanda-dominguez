Hey there, my name is Fernanda, you could say I wasn’t born to fit in a box, I wanted to study piano for a living. I got in a theater bachelor’s degree, until I got depressed but got really good, discover a call in singing but also scenography and makeup. It was a mess. My urge to create couldn’t really fit in a scenery. (Well not in Mexico).
So, Where could I find a canvas for creativity and urge. Yes… Fashion.

I changed to a Fashion Design Bachelor’s degree, in the Laureates University, UVM en Mexico’s city. Sociology, tendencies, collections, runways, fashion events, had so much fun. Made my greatest friends.
I, then started an editorial inclined platform to organize Mexico’s mess to get supplies. Boomer, that makes creation a lot slower :( Taking fashion photos, and looking for suppliers., was born Vuzzola, compass in Italian. I develop PR skills and got in great shows, and started collaborating with bigger firms. More that a magazine, I wanted to deliver a more tech way to share this.
Decided to make the web-app, I meet with a developer, presented the idea, he agreed to partner with the project but asked me for the interphase design… and I was there, designing an UI interphase for the first time in my life.

It was fun, creative and useful. The developer disappeared and I got distracted launching my fashion brand called Domínguez México, a leather’s good original design brand (not re-sellers charade). Found investment with a former professor from the University, develop the product and started selling. Urged to make my webpage I become keen in finding the most suitable way to develop my webpage, went through WIX, WordPress, and HTML CSS. Youtube on one hand and on the other on my non-musical keyboard.
I found my calling, developing web solutions, from the design of the interphase to some whole developments.
I enjoy the collaboration with other developers and designers, but also going solo it’s so fun.
I’m always learning new ways to solve things, and the story ends handsomely, I finally found the most unlimited form of creation. < Coding >
