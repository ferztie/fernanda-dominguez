Bodyssage

Web Development

Bodyssage and Face Move are specialized services that offer sculptural massages and facial gymnastics.

Their unique identity required visits to the location and several sessions with inspirational moodboards to identify the true technological-craftsmanship spirit of Bodyssage and Face Move. The services are designed for users who value effective and specialized attention for their health and beauty, even with limited time. I was responsible for the integral management of this responsive website, from domain and hosting to UXR and UI design and enabling features. The project is constantly being iterated to improve the UX.
The site is hosted on WP and is completely manageable by Mona, the owner, so she can upload new posts and products at any time.
