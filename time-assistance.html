Time Assistance Services

Web Development

Health and funeral assistance services.

Time Assistance requested the corporate identity for their SkyGarden product and later requested a redesign of their own company name and logo.

This project, currently in the bidding process, presents new challenges such as telemedicine, building a community, and developing an app.
I'll keep you updated on the progress of the project.
