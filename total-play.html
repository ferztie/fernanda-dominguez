TotalPlay

Web Development

TotalPlay is an important high-quality entertainment and connectivity platform with cutting-edge technology, and it is top-of-mind in Mexico.

FFM Cloud is a tool and product for TotalPlay Empresarial that allows for the visualization of quotes and breakdowns of customer accounts from work orders, in order to trigger different flows and operations.
What's most interesting about FFM Cloud is its gradual growth, which translates into innovation and new tools, such as its online store, its OnDemand workshop management site, its donation manager, and more.
The project is still ongoing with the goal of continuing to grow and expand its functionalities. This reflects TotalPlay's commitment to staying at the forefront of technology and offering innovative solutions to its customers. The platform's ability to adapt to the changing needs of businesses and consumers is a key factor in its success, and it serves as a testament to the power of innovation in the tech industry.
